Exercise in 2023

In 2023, I used an Apple Watch to track my workouts. At the beginning of the year, my weight shot up, reaching its peak around March. I started taking cycling classes at Super Monkey and began swimming in June.

However, I injured my knee due to issues with my swimming technique, leading me to stop exercising for a month or two. Afterwards, I resumed swimming, primarily focusing on freestyle, and incorporated simple home workouts. Gradually, I lost over ten kilograms. Through exercising, I discovered its positive impact on my emotions and body shaping. It brought me joy, a sense of immersion, and freedom.
